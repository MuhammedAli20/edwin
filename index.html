EIT is a Masdar based company in Abu Dhabi focusing on transitional technologies & industrialization.

By recognizing transitional bottlenecks, EIT is focusing on rapid industrialization and commercialization of advanced manufacturing in order to help facilitate the transition to Net Zero. Founded by industry experts and veterans with a strong track record, EIT’s vision is to build a green energy manufacturing ecosystem in the Middle East.

ESG is at the heart of our business. EIT has identified three ESG priorities:

Protecting environment

carbon footprint and
circular economy

Building responsible partners

ethical supply chain, and transparency with
stakeholders

Upskilling and Emiratization

building capacity in the
community

Regarding carbon footprint our processes will enable us to reduce CO2 emissions in our value chain by ~60% from the industry benchmark.
