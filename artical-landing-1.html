Edwin Institute of Technology (EIT)

EIT is a Masdar based company in Abu Dhabi focusing on transitional technologies and industrialization. By recognizing transitional bottlenecks, EIT is focusing on rapid industrialization and commercialization of advanced manufacturing in order to help facilitate the transition to Net Zero. Formed by industry experts and veterans with a strong track record, EIT’s vision is to build a green energy manufacturing ecosystem in the Middle East.

Mission:

EIT makes the best use of strong geopolitical dynamics to put UAE and Abu Dhabi on the map as the leading nation focusing on transitional investments paving the way for a true green sustainable economy. Our mission is to accelerate greenfication by creating sticky IP for the next generation. EIT delivers best in class high performance batteries specifically tailored for luxury sports car and aviation segments.

How we do it:

Battery manufacturing scale up can turn into a costly and complex business. Meanwhile mainstream EV market is becoming overly price competitive making battery production viable only if it’s produced at very large scales (10s GWh). EIT has overcome this challenge by focusing on niche markets with less price sensitivity. Simultaneously by owning the manufacturing landscape and employing innovative production solutions to bring capital and operational expenditure down. With higher sales prices and lower equipment & production cost, suddenly, small Gigafactories “asset light battery manufacturing” becomes financially viable. (profitability delta picture)

EIT R&D centre:

The 600 sqm facilities is housing EIT pilot production line, testing and research capabilities. The centre has three main objectives:

  • Material screening and design/formulation development
  • Material screening and design/formulation development
  • Research and development on next generation of batteries and IP creation

EIT R&D centre plays a critical role in commercialization and technology transfer of high performance batteries in UAE.
